## Target Audience

The portfolio targets three distinct client types, each with different needs. The "Who It's For" section of the site addresses them directly:

### 1. Startups
- **Who:** Founders and early-stage product teams (pre-seed to Series A)
- **Pain point:** Need investor-ready, user-validated design but can't afford a full in-house team
- **What they want:** Fast, credible, dev-ready output — MVP screens, prototypes, design system foundations
- **Key message:** "Get investor-ready, user-validated design — without hiring a full team."
- **Accent colour:** Emerald / Green

### 2. Growth-Stage Companies
- **Who:** Product leads and engineering managers at scaling companies (Series B+)
- **Pain point:** Design is becoming a bottleneck — inconsistency across platforms, slow handoffs, no system
- **What they want:** Design infrastructure (design systems, tokens, multi-platform consistency) that lets the team ship faster
- **Key message:** "Build the design infrastructure that lets your team ship faster."
- **Accent colour:** Blue / Cyan

### 3. Enterprise
- **Who:** Directors of UX, VPs of Product, or programme owners at large organisations
- **Pain point:** Stakeholder complexity, compliance requirements (WCAG), slow decision-making
- **What they want:** Design leadership — governance models, accessibility compliance, stakeholder alignment, executive-ready deliverables
- **Key message:** "Design leadership that handles stakeholder complexity and compliance."
- **Accent colour:** Violet / Purple

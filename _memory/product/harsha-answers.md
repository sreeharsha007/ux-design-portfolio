# Harsha's Answers — Product Owner Input

Collected April 2026. These are direct answers from Harsha (product owner) and serve as ground truth for all audit agents.

---

## Session 1 — Goals & Metrics

**Q: How many new freelance clients/month is a success?**
No fixed monthly target. Goal is a steady stream of inbound as needed. Not chasing volume — quality of projects matters more.

**Q: Typical project value?**
- Now: Under $5k and $5k–$15k range
- Target within 6 months: $15k–$50k engagements
- Direction of travel is upward over time

**Q: Engagement model preference?**
Open to all — fixed-scope projects, monthly retainers, consulting/advisory, and Upwork hourly engagements. No preference at this stage.

**Q: Which audience tier to prioritise in the next 90 days?**
Startups and Growth-stage companies, equally. Enterprise is a longer-term play.

## Session 3 — Contact & Positioning

**Q: What name replaces "Your Name"?**
**Harsha** — first name only.

**Q: How do you frame the AI angle with clients?**
Depends on the client:
- Startups and growth-stage: Lead with **outcomes** (faster, cheaper, better results)
- Technical clients: They're interested in **tools and process** — explain the AI stack
- This should be reflected in how the site communicates to different tiers

**Q: Preferred contact flow?**
All three channels (email, direct booking via Calendly, Upwork profile) should be present. For now, primary contact paths are **email first** and **Upwork**. Calendly/booking is secondary.

**Q: Visual aesthetic direction?**
- Current "AI tech" dark aesthetic feels **too generic** — wants something more minimal
- Trusts the Art Director to make the call based on the brand brief
- No specific visual references provided

## Session 4 — Metrics, Capacity & Brand

**Q: Are the hero metrics (40% faster, 20+ projects, 5.0★) real?**
**All aspirational / placeholder.** Not backed by real data currently. Open to ideating on whether metrics even add value or should be replaced with something else.

**Q: Concurrent project capacity?**
Depends on project size:
- 1 large enterprise project at a time
- OR 3–4 smaller startup/growth projects simultaneously

**Q: Does the three-tier segmentation (Startup / Growth / Enterprise) match how clients describe themselves?**
No — it's Harsha's framing of their needs, not language clients use. Clients describe **problems**, not stages. The site uses the tier labels for clarity but this may need to be reconsidered in copy.

**Q: Dark mode vs light mode?**
- Trust the Art Director's judgement
- Both dark and light should be equally polished — enterprise and corporate clients will likely use light mode, so it cannot be an afterthought

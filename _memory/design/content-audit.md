## Writing Standards for This Project

- **Tone:** Confident, clear, expert but human (see `global/brand/harsha-brand-guidelines.md`)
- **CTA buttons:** Action verbs first — "View case study", "Get in touch", "See my work"
- **Case study headlines:** Focus on the outcome — "How I reduced onboarding drop-off by 40%"
- **Bio copy:** First person, specific, no generic phrases like "passionate designer" or "love solving problems"
